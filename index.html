MEET YOUR EVENT PLANNER

Hello there! I’m Janelle Gaston, founder of First Class Decor’. We are a small creative business dedicated to helping you design and host a stunning event for the ones you love. As an avid decorator and organizer, I am beyond excited to plan your wedding, milestone birthday celebration, extravagant launch party, or a classic holiday soiree.

I take every opportunity to turn a simple occasion or dinner among friends into an efficient, enjoyable and stylish event. I can offer as little or as much insight and assistance as you need, with the same goal always at heart - to help you and your guests have the best time without needing to worry if something has been overlooked in the background (I got you covered!). I put an emphasis on delivering high quality, consistent results alongside friendly, upbeat and inspired service.

Let First Class Decor' make your perfect day possible!

SERVICES
